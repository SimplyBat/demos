The Problem

Every year, humanity produces over 500 billion plastic bottles — more than one million every minute. Only 9 % are recycled. Over 2 billion tons of global waste choke our oceans and landfills, costing cities millions.

2 B+

tons of waste per year

The Solution

AI Sorting Facilities

Using AlexNet-powered computer vision, high-speed cameras classify plastics, metals, glass, paper, and e-waste. Robotic arms separate materials in real-time, achieving 95 %+ accuracy and cutting contamination by half.

Reverse Vending Machines (RVMs)

Customers return bottles and cans, receiving instant cash or rewards. AI verifies material type and brand, sending data and materials to local sorting hubs.
