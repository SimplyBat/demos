Problem

Global waste overload: over 2 billion tons of waste yearly and 500+ billion plastic bottles produced annually — only ~9% recycled. Landfills, oceans, and microplastics damage ecosystems and public health.

Our Solution

A unified ecosystem combining:

  • AI Sorting Facilities — AlexNet-powered computer vision + robotic sorters that classify plastics, metals, glass, paper, and e-waste in real time.
  • Reverse Vending Machines (RVMs) — consumer kiosks that accept bottles/cans and pay instant cash or digital rewards; items are routed to facilities.
  • Logistics & Data — pickup, processing, and analytics sold as services to cities and brands.

How It Works

  1. Consumers drop bottles/cans into RVMs → receive cash.
  2. RVMs store & report materials; collected by logistics partners.
  3. Mixed inputs go to AI Sorting Facilities: cameras → AlexNet classification → robotic separation.
  4. Clean streams sold to buyers (aluminum, PET, paper, glass, e-waste refineries).

Buyers & Revenue

  • Material resale (aluminum, copper, PET, HDPE, glass, paper, e-waste metals)
  • RVM leasing & hosting fees (retailers, malls, campuses)
  • AI licensing & plant integration
  • Advertising & data analytics for corporate ESG

Impact & Advantages

  • Divert millions of bottles from landfills and oceans
  • Boost recycling efficiency and material value
  • Reduce emissions and landfill costs
  • Increase store foot traffic and brand ESG credibility
  • Automate hazardous manual sorting for worker safety
