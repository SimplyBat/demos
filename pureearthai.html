How It Works

1. RVMs placed at stores, campuses, transit hubs accept bottles & cans; customers receive instant cash or digital payout. AI verifies material and reduces contamination at source.

2. Collection & Logistics: RVMs feed collected loads to regional PureEarth AI hubs on optimized routes.

3. AI Sorting Facilities: High-speed cameras + AlexNet models classify plastics (PET, HDPE, LDPE, PVC), metals (aluminum, steel, copper), glass (clear/green/amber), paper/cardboard, and e-waste; robotic actuators separate materials in real time.

Why Cities & Retailers Pay

  • Foot traffic: RVMs bring customers who redeem cash and shop.
  • ESG & Compliance: Helps meet recycling mandates and qualify for grants.
  • New revenue: Recyclables become high-purity sellable resources.
