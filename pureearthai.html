AI Sorting Facilities

Using AlexNet-powered computer vision, PureEarth AI facilities classify and separate plastics, metals, paper, glass, and e-waste in real time — increasing material purity and resale value.

Reverse Vending Machines (RVMs)

Consumers deposit bottles and cans, earn instant cash, and feed the AI network that directs materials to facilities. RVMs attract foot traffic for retailers and help cities meet recycling mandates.
